# 8-Puzzle Solver Experiment Results

## Heuristics Used

### Heuristic 1: Misplaced Tiles
Counts the number of tiles that are not in their goal position.

### Heuristic 2: Manhattan Distance
Sums the Manhattan distance (|x1 - x2| + |y1 - y2|) for each tile from its current position to its goal position.

### Heuristic 3: Linear Conflict
Combines Manhattan distance with a penalty for linear conflicts. A linear conflict occurs when two tiles are in their goal row/column but in the wrong order.
